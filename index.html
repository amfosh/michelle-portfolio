Michelle Meier

Michelle Meier is a writer, editor, photographer, and educator. She is the author of "Famous Geranium" (Nauset Press, 2015), a Pushcart Prize and Best of the Net nominee, a recipient of fellowships at The Saltonstall Foundation and The Hambidge Center, and a former poetry editor and art editor of Foundry. Her written work has appeared or is forthcoming in Michigan Quarterly Review, Conduit, Denver Quarterly, The Journal, The Rumpus, Radar Poetry, Pool Poetry, Dialogist and elsewhere. She lives in New York.
